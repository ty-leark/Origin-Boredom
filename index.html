I AM BORED...

I created this neighborhood service system simply because I was bored and I saw a need. I'm 26(M) and live at 122 Laurel Wood. I have a deep appreciation for preparation, precision, and the impact of small details, perhaps because of my service in the Navy. I know firsthand what it’s like to wake up on a freezing morning, late for work and rushing to get out the door with no one around to assist. It’s often the little things that throw people off before their day even begins. That’s why I created a system to offer a range of practical, military-grade neighborhood services designed to make mornings smoother and lives easier. From steamed clothes and clean shoes to a warm car with a clear windshield, my goal is to make life run a little cleaner, warmer, and sharper for my neighbors...brought to you by boredom.

-Tylik Mawyer
